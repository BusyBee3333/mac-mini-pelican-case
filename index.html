Design Features

Debossed Apple Logo
0.8mm deep precision deboss centred on the lid top — visible, elegant, unmistakable
Compact Form Factor
Only 4.2mm per side larger than the Mac Mini itself — minimal bulk, maximum protection
Fully Sealed
No port holes, no ventilation — this is a transport case, not an operating enclosure
Pelican-Style Latches
Two front snap latches with pivoting arms — secure positive closure
Piano Hinges
Interlocking knuckle hinges on the rear accept M2.5 pin rod for smooth open/close
Bolted Arch Handle
Separate printed piece with comfort grip grooves — mounts with M4 bolts through lid
Interior Cradle Ribs
Precision support rails hold the Mac Mini centred — no foam needed
Pressure Equalization
2mm valve hole in rear wall prevents vacuum seal — lid opens smoothly every time
Tongue & Groove Seal
Alignment ledge at split line for precise closure and dust resistance
Luxury Accent Groove
Thin perimeter groove around all four faces — subtle detail that elevates the look

Print & Assembly

Material

  • PETG or ABS recommended — latches need flex resistance
  • Matte black PETG for the premium look
  • PLA works but latch arms may snap over time

Settings

  • Layer height: 0.2mm (0.12mm for crisper Apple logo)
  • Infill: 20-30% gyroid
  • Walls: 3-4 perimeters
  • Supports: NOT needed — all parts print flat
  • Estimated: ~3-4 hours bottom, ~2-3 hours lid, ~1 hour handle

Assembly

  • Thread M2.5 rod (or 1.75mm filament) through rear hinge knuckles
  • Bolt handle to lid with 2× M4 bolts + nuts (through lid holes)
  • Optional: press rubber feet into bottom recesses
  • Optional: sand 220-800 grit + matte clear coat for show-floor finish
Designed for Mac Mini M4 (2024) — 127 × 127 × 50mm. Clearance is 1.2mm per side. If your printer over-extrudes, scale cavity by 100.5% or sand contact surfaces.
